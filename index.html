Privacy Policy

Impulse Control • Effective February 6, 2026

Short version: Impulse Control does not require an account. Your data stays on your device. Ads (if shown) are non‑personalized.

What data I collect

Impulse Control is designed to work without creating an account. I do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or payment details inside the app.

Data stored on your device

Your items, decisions, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple’s built‑in storage. I do not upload this data to a server.

Ads (non‑personalized)

If you are using the free version, the app may show ads from Google AdMob. These ads are non‑personalized and may use limited device data (such as device identifiers or approximate location) to deliver relevant ads and measure performance.

You can remove ads at any time by upgrading to Pro.

Tracking & App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

Impulse Control does not use tracking for advertising. The app does not prompt for App Tracking Transparency permission.

Third‑party services

The app may use:

  • Google AdMob for non‑personalized ads (free version only).
  • Apple StoreKit for in‑app purchases.

Children’s privacy

Impulse Control is not directed at children under 13, and I do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If I update this policy, I will post the new version on this page and update the effective date above.

Contact
